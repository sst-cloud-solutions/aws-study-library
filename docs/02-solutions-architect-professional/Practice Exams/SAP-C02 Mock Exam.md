## Tips for the Exam
1. **Analyze Constraints:** Pay special attention to keywords like *lowest latency*, *lowest cost*, *minimal operational overhead*, and *near-zero downtime* to eliminate valid but sub-optimal solutions.
2. **Review Explanations:** Every question contains a collapsible section containing the correct answer, architectural details, and explanations of why other distractors are incorrect. Use this to reinforce your design decisions.
