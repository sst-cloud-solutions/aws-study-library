## 🎯 Quick Recommendations by Scenario

### Scenario: "Exam is in 6 hours, help!"
1. ULTRA-QUICK-REFERENCE-CARD.md (15 min)
2. MEMORY-CARDS.md - Top 20 cards only (15 min)
3. Skim "Must Know Numbers" from ALL-TESTS-MASTER (10 min)
4. Review your weakest domain summary (10 min)
5. Take a break, stay calm!

### Scenario: "I keep failing High-Performing domain"
1. Complete Test 1 Review - High-Performing section (1 hr)
2. Complete Test 5 Review - High-Performing section (1 hr)
3. ALL-TESTS-MASTER → ECS, Lambda, Caching sections (45 min)
4. Practice questions on those topics (1 hr)

### Scenario: "I have all day to study"

3. Lunch Break
4. Afternoon: Complete reviews of failed tests (4 hrs)
5. Evening: MEMORY-CARDS (30 min)

2. ULTRA-QUICK-REFERENCE-CARD.md (tables/lists - 20 min)

3. Focus on weak domains using master guide
4. Take next practice test to confirm improvement
